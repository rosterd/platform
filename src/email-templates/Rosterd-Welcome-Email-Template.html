Frequently Asked Questions

Your organization {{organization}} has registered with the Rosterd platform and an administrator from your organization has added you to get access to the Rosterd app.

How does it all work?
Every time there is a shift that needs to be covered or a spare shift is available, this comes to the Rosterd app as a job.

You can setup alerts to be notified for the type of jobs and the date/times that you prefer.

How many jobs can I accept?
As many as you can do, there is no limit as long as you can manage the workload.
